NANI - Baby Scheduler

NANI (Newborn Application for Nursing Itinerary) is a data entry form
that records the date of putting your baby to sleep,
feeding them, and when you change their diaper.
It also allows for recording temperature, heart rate,
respiratory rate, weight, and height.
Built out of a need to record data for me and my husband's
newborn, we aim to make parenting data-driven.

The data is stored in Google Sheets.
